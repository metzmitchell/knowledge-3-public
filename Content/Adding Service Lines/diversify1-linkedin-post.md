# LinkedIn Post - Adding Service Lines: Brand Architecture Decision

Adding a service line breaks your old brand strategy.
It forces a choice: one brand that does everything, or separate brands that speak clearly.
In construction, I see two models work:
Branded house: One site, one name, all your services. Trust compounds, but the message gets broad.
House of brands: Separate sites and names for each buyer segment. Clearer message, more cost and complexity.
