# Current Work & Business - Mitch Metz Marketing

## Executive Summary

Mitch Metz Marketing is a fractional CMO and marketing advisory firm launched January 1, 2025, serving home builders and remodelers across the United States. With 10 years of marketing experience and recognition as the 2023 and 2025 Marketing Professional of the Year by the Builders Association of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Mitch helps builders and remodelers transition from referral-dependent marketing to building sustainable in-house marketing capabilities. The business operates with a transparent, strategic consulting model that includes selective execution support, with expertise in AI and automation integration beyond common use cases.
