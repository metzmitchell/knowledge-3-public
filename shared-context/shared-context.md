## Mitch Metz - Complete Profile

### Professional Background
Mitch is a fractional CMO and marketing advisor for the homebuilding and remodeling industry with 10 years of marketing experience. As a former Marketing Director at Sullivan Family of Companies (2022-2024), he earned the 2023 and 2025 Marketing Professional of the Year awards from the Builders Association of the Blue Ridge Mountains. With a foundation in technical production from his Bachelor's degree in Motion Picture & Television Production, he has built a comprehensive marketing career spanning film, digital marketing, and construction industries since 2014. He is educated about AI and automation building, integrating AI into businesses beyond common use cases, constantly adapting and adopting technology to stay competitive.

## Business Philosophy

He strongly prefers project-based consulting over traditional employment, focusing on quality over quantity in client relationships. His approach emphasizes strategic consulting with selective execution, particularly in the custom home builder and luxury brand space. Mitch operates without non-compete restrictions and maintains a clear vision for building a sellable business with scalable systems.

## Personal Interests

An avid science fiction enthusiast, Mitch enjoys complex narratives like the Murderbot Diaries and Three-Body Problem. He's skilled at Super Mario World speedrunning and has extensive experience in video production using DaVinci Resolve. His technical interests extend to AI development, workflow optimization, and system automation using tools like n8n and OpenAI.

## Learning & Communication Style

Mitch prefers learning through Socratic questioning and practical application. He values advanced insights over basics and emphasizes action over planning. His organizational approach focuses on documented processes for AI integration, and he actively participates in professional groups including the Blue Ridge Mountains Builders Association.

## Business Overview
Mitch runs Mitch Metz Marketing, launched in January 2025. His value proposition is: "I help builders and remodelers get projects more consistently than referrals and with more transparency than an agency." He operates as a fractional CMO and marketing advisor, focusing on strategic consulting with selective execution to build a marketing foundation within his clients' companies. Services include strategic consulting, fractional CMO services, and productized marketing plans.

## Target Audience & Market
His primary market is home builders and remodelers across the United States. The ideal company has a marketing person on their team, or someone where marketing is part of their job. Clients need a budget to invest in their marketing of around $10,000 per month at least. Key personas within this demographic are hands-on Owners, Office Managers tasked with marketing, and growth-focused Sales Directors. These clients typically value industry reputation and are seeking to evolve beyond a purely referral-based lead generation model.

The industry includes diverse demographics:
- Production builders
- Pre-fab modular builders
- Custom home builders
- Luxury custom home builders
- 55+ builders
- High-end remodelers

Current focus is on remodelers who also do a few other services, with plans to expand strategies for other builder types.
