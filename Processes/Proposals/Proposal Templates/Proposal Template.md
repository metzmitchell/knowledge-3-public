# Proposal Template

This folder contains the core templates and guidelines for writing proposals.

## Core Documents

- **[Section Differentiation](Section%20Differentiation.md)** - Guidance on exactly what content belongs in which section of a proposal.

- **[Writing Style](Writing%20Style.md)** - Proposal-specific tone instructions, including nuanced language, empathetic authority, and honest uncertainty.

- **[Retainer Proposal Template](Retainer%20Proposal%20Template.md)** - The mandatory structural format to follow when writing proposals.

- **[Proposal Mindset](Proposal%20Mindset.md)** - Core philosophy: verification, co-buyer stance, pricing psychology.

## How to Use

1. Review [Proposal Writing SOP](../Proposal%20Writing%20SOP.md) for the complete process.
2. Use the [Retainer Proposal Template](Retainer%20Proposal%20Template.md) as your structural guide.
3. Reference [Section Differentiation](Section%20Differentiation.md) to ensure content is in the right place.
4. Apply [Writing Style](Writing%20Style.md) guidelines for tone and voice.
5. Follow [Proposal Mindset](Proposal%20Mindset.md) principles throughout.
