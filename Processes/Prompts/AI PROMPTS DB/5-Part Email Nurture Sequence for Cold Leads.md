# 5-Part Email Nurture Sequence for Cold Leads

Prompt: You are an expert copywriter. Create a 5-part email nurture sequence that takes a cold lead and warms them up to consider buying my service. The sequence should follow this flow: 1) Introduction and problem awareness, 2) Build trust with a story or authority, 3) Educate with valuable insights, 4) Position the offer as a solution, and 5) A strong, clear CTA to book a call or buy. Each email should include a subject line, preview text, body copy (around 150-200 words), and a call-to-action. Make it emotionally intelligent and value-driven. Inputs: Product/service, target audience, biggest pain points, transformation promised, brand tone. Example Inputs: Productivity course for busy creators
