# Marketing Minds: Ethical Marketing with AI

We're gathering marketers and entrepreneurs who want to sharpen their skills in a casual collaborative learning environment.

This time, Sumaya Owens will be facilitating.

She'll cover ethical marketing with AI. From understanding the basic rules that guide good AI marketing choices to putting those ideas into practice, we'll explore how to use AI tools while keeping real human connections. The session will range from looking at current AI marketing tools and their ethical issues to building frameworks, picking the right technologies, and using them responsibly.

A bit about Sumaya:

Sumaya Owens (she/they) brings nearly twenty years of digital marketing experience. She focuses on growing brands in health, wellness, and social impact areas.

She founded and runs Present Moment Media. This agency helps healers, activists, visionaries, and changemakers with their digital presence.

Sumaya cares deeply about ethical marketing, diversity, equity, and inclusion. She weaves these into her strategies for content, social media, ads, emails, and launches.

Beyond marketing, she's a trauma-informed yoga teacher and mental health advocate. She champions embodied leadership and community-focused business practices.

Sumaya has worked as a Fractional CMO and consulted widely. She studied Marketing Management at Western Governors University and trained with JB Media in Asheville.

These experiences make her perfect for talking about values-driven AI in marketing.
